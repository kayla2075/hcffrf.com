HCFFRF Membership

Becoming a member makes you family!

Join our family! We're dedicated to assisting fire and rescue workers who provide services to the visitors and residents of Horry County. We help each other either financially or by lending assistance of services provided by the contributors to the fund or services by supporters. Our membership program was established so immediate assistance may be made available, please help us sustain our resources by becoming a member. Please fill out the membership application form below to complete your member registration process.
