Annual HCFFRF Bowling Tournament

Mark your calendars and come join us for the annual HCFFRF bowling tournament. This year we will be holding it on Sunday, March 8th (2015) with registration from 12-1pm and bowling from 1-4pm. It's a family friendly event so bring everyone. $25 (for adults) and $15 (for children 12 and under) covers 3 games of bowling, shoes, hot dogs, chips, cookies, soda and for the adults 1 pitcher of beer per lane. A portion of the proceeds goes to benefit the Relief Fund. We've had a great time in the past few years and we hope this year is even better. Door prizes, 50/50 and fun for all!!
