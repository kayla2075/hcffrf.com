Our Mission

The Horry County Firefighters Relief Fund was organized in the Fall of 2009 and since has assisted many firefighters and their families in great times of need.

As a 501C (3) non-profit organization, the fund is dedicated to assisting and improving the lives of Horry County firefighters, rescue workers, their families, and the communities they serve. Whether affected by injury or life threatening illness, our goal is to provide as much relief as possible.

Several fund raisers are held each year, to include but not limited to Bowling Tournaments, Golf Tournaments, and Raffle Sales.

The Horry County Firefighter Relief Fund provides assistance to the following community service events: Myrtle Beach Harley Davidsonâ€™s 9/11 Memorial Poker Run, the Conway Christmas Parade, and the Aynor Hoedown. Each summer, the fund sponsors two children to be sent to the Medical University of South Carolina (MUSC) Burn Camp. This opportunity allows these children to participate in activities geared towards creating positive self-images while being surrounded by peers who share similar medical experiences.

The fund works closely with the Horry County Fire Rescue Department and provides refreshments, as needed, when units are on the scene of emergencies and fires. Donating to this worthy cause is the best investment one could make and we humbly ask for you to consider making a contribution at this time.
