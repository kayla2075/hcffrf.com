Welcome to Horry County Firefighters Relief Fund

Horry County Firefighters Relief Fund is dedicated to serve because there is nothing more important than our families and giving hope to those who need it!! We thank all of our sponsors and volunteers for
making this non-profit 501c(3) organization possible each year.

This is what we're all about

  • Assisting Horry County's Firefighters
  • Assisting Horry County's Firefighters' families
  • Making emergency funds/resources available to our firefighters when needed
  • Sponsoring two Horry County children on a trip to burn camp annually
  • Donating our time, skills, and resources to our family of firefighters and the surrounding community
  • Hosting a Bowling Tournament each year
  • Hosting a Golf Tournament each year
  • Participating in the Aynor Hoedown each year
  • Participating in the Conway Christmas Parade Each Year
  • Assisting with the Myrtle Beach Harley Davidson's 9/11 memorial poker run

Please consider helping out by donating to the Horry County Firefighters Relief Fund. Donations of any size are incredibly helpful to are cause. It is one of the best investments that you could make.
